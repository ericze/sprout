# Sprout Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-28

Sprout stores baby records locally first. Core records remain available on the device even when network features are unavailable.

If you sign in and enable cloud sync, the app may upload records, sync metadata, and related assets required to keep your account-backed data in sync. Cloud sync is designed not to delete local data when a sync fails.

We do not use baby records for advertising. Any AI or assistant capability must be presented clearly before it is used and must not become a prerequisite for core recording.

For privacy questions, contact the app developer through the App Store support channel associated with this app.
