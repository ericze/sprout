# Sprout Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026-04-28

Sprout is a local-first baby record app. Core recording features are designed to work without a network connection or a subscription.

Paid Pro capabilities are only offered in the app after they pass release checks and are shown in the paywall as available. Features marked as coming soon are not sold as active subscription benefits.
